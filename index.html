Epoxy Flooring

Durable, hygienic and easy to clean – ideal for garages, kitchens, warehouses, schools and more. Systems include 100% solids epoxy base, coloured coats, optional vinyl flakes, and polyurethane/polyaspartic clear coats.

How we apply epoxy
  1. Prepare the slab: deep clean, repair cracks/holes, grind or acid‑etch; ensure dry.
  2. Prime: choose a compatible epoxy/moisture‑block primer; roll on evenly and allow to dry.
  3. Mix resin + hardener: follow ratios; mix thoroughly to ensure proper curing.
  4. Base coat: roll a uniform coat; level edges; allow 12–24h to set.
  5. Top coat & flakes: apply protective top coat; optionally broadcast vinyl flakes; let cure 2–3 days.
Care & maintenance
  • Regular sweep/vacuum; mop with warm water and pH‑neutral cleaner.
  • Wipe spills promptly; avoid abrasive pads and harsh chemicals.
  • Re‑seal every 3–5 years in high‑traffic or UV‑exposed areas.

Pressure Washing

High‑pressure cleaning to remove dirt, mould and grime from driveways, paths, patios and more. We select the right nozzles, detergent and pressure for each surface.

Typical workflow
  • Sweep/prepare area; protect doors, windows and plants.
  • Set up washer and apply a concrete‑safe cleaning solution.
  • Wash from high to low, overlapping passes for even coverage.
  • Rinse thoroughly; allow to dry and inspect. Optional sealing.
Safety matters

Gloves, eye protection and closed‑toe shoes are used. Pressure is adjusted to suit the substrate to prevent damage.
